Movingeasy

The story started 13 years ago. In 2002 no movers existed on Sakhalin. When expats started to arrive they realized that nobody was able to help with handling of household goods at all. It is much better now, however there is still no one with such a background as Moving Easy (ME). The founder of Moving Easy (ME) has 20 years' experience in shipping and removals and 13 years' experience in the area of Sakhalin/Russian Far East. Through the last decade all staff was thoroughly selected and trained according to the international removals standards. High qualification of our employees coupled with no staff turnover always brings good results in the end.
We can pack anything that a house or an office contains. It is important to feel the object with hands and if you do so the object arrives absolutely safe to a new place. We not only pack – we can move your house or office no matter the size, the volume and the distance. We organize transportation worldwide. Moreover, we store at a modern and safe storage well located not far from the City Centre.
